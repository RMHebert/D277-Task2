Interesting Historical Factoids

  • 1620: French explorers discover Lake Superior.
  • 1668: Sault Ste. Marie is founded. It is the first permanent settlement in Michigan.
  • 1763: After winning the French and Indian War, the British acquire Michigan.
  • 1792: Michigan holds its first elections.
  • 1835: Michigan and Ohio fight the Toledo War.
  • 1837: Michigan is incorporated into the Union.
  • 1896: Henry Ford builds his first automobile.
  • 1935: The United Auto Workers union is formed.
  • 1959: Berry Gordy founds Motown Records.
Source: Michigan: Unique facts, interesting history about the Greak Lakes State. Detroit Free Press.
